The Refreshing Story of Refresh!

As a young girl, Nina Thomson never had the privilege of entering hair salons and experiencing the pleasure of having ready to go hair stylists who could cater to her curlier hair type. This simple experience inspired the beginning of "Refresh" —a newly refreshing hair salon catered to each and every hair type, with the aim to serve customers with nothing but good vibes and beautiful tips that are sure to raise your confidence and leave you refreshed.

With a beauty tips section, an endless photo gallery to help you decide your next great hair style and staff at the ready to bring out the best new you in every hairdo, Refresh is guaranteed to bring out a new, fresh experience for clients.

Our Mission!

It is of our utmost goal to ensure that each client can happily walk out of our salon with the acknowledgement that they feel as beautiful inside, as they are outside. A comfortable and pleasing experince is what we aim to provide for our clients while you find the new you in your new hairdo.
